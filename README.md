
# Student Performance Analysis – Power BI 


## Project Overview


This project analyzes the relationship between Artificial Intelligence (AI) usage and student academic performance using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into study habits, AI dependency, attendance, academic scores, and overall student performance. The goal is to understand how AI tools influence learning outcomes and educational success.🚀

## Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

Analyze student academic performance.
Examine AI usage patterns among students.
Measure the impact of AI dependency on academic results.
Identify factors influencing student success.
Provide actionable insights for educators and students.

## Dashborad Visuvalization

1. KPI Cards

Display overall student statistics and performance indicators.

2. AI Users Distribution (Pie Chart)

Shows the proportion of students using AI versus non-AI users.

3. Performance Category Distribution

Displays the distribution of Low, Medium, and High performers.

4. Final Score by Grade Level

Compares academic performance across different grade levels.

5. AI Usage Purpose Analysis

Shows how students use AI tools for learning activities.

6. AI Tools Usage Analysis

Highlights the most frequently used AI tools.

7. AI Dependency vs Final Score (Scatter Chart)

Analyzes the relationship between AI dependency and academic performance.

8. Study Hours vs Final Score

Measures the effect of study habits on academic success.

9. Gender-wise Performance Analysis

Compares performance distribution among different genders.

## Key Insights

Students with higher study hours generally achieve better academic results.

Moderate AI usage can support learning and improve productivity.

Excessive AI dependency may negatively affect concept understanding.

Attendance has a significant influence on final scores.

Students with consistent study habits tend to perform better academically.

AI tools are commonly used for notes preparation, coding assistance, homework, and exam preparation.

## Conclusion

The Student AI Usage and Academic Performance Dashboard provides valuable insights into the relationship between AI-assisted learning and academic achievement. By combining academic, behavioral, and AI-related factors, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making for students, educators, and institutions. The project demonstrates how Power BI can transform educational data into meaningful and actionable insights.
